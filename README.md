  <h3>Hey there! I'm Vamsi</h3>
  I'm a machine learning developer who spends most of my time at the intersection of models, data, and the occasional over-engineered side project. I like taking research-y ideas — RAG pipelines, offline LLMs, sentiment models — and turning them into things people can actually click on. When I'm not training something or debugging a pipeline at 1am, I'm probably deep in a LeetCode streak or elbow-deep in a Kaggle notebook that started as "quick EDA" and became something else entirely.
